How to Play

Solve the clues and join the solutions together to form famous quotations from poems, novels, or films. Clues could be antonyms or synonyms of the quote, quick crossword, or cryptic crossword clues. Puzzles get harder throughout the week, i.e., may include a mix of clue types and less help offered (e.g., emoji, clue type, etc.).
